Ingredients (2 baguettes)

  • 500 g of unbleached flour
  • 300 mL of warm water
  • 10 g of kosher salt
  • 14 g of dry or fresh yeast
  • 1 tbsp of olive oil

Materials

  • Large stainless steel bowl
  • Baking tray or baguette mold
  • Measuring cup
  • Kitchen towel
  • Sharp knife or serrated scissors

Heat oven to 425 ℉
and prepare ingredients.

Mix the flour and salt.

Create a hole in the center
of the flour-salt mixture.

Add the yeast to the center.

Add warm water to
dissolve the yeast.

Slowly incorporate the
flour-salt mixture into
the yeast solution.

Continue to mix the
ingredients until a
dough is formed.

Vigorously knead the
dough for 6 minutes.

Place the dough into
a bowl coated with a
thin layer of olive oil.

Cover bowl with towel
and let sit in a warm
place for at least 2
hours.

The dough should
have doubled in size.

Remove dough from
bowl and place on
lightly floured surface.

Flatten the dough and
shape it into a square.

Fold the sides of the
square towards the center
and cut in 1/2 to create 2
cylinders.

Stretch the dough 'til
doubled in length and
create pointed edges.

Place baguettes on
baking sheet.

Let sit 1.5 hours in
a warm place until
doubled in size.

Make diagonal slashes
on the surface using
knife or scissors.

Bake in oven for 20 to
25 min. with hot water
in a cake tin.

Baguettes are done
when there's a golden
crust.
